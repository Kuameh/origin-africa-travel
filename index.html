Our Story

Where Africa begins

Origin Africa Travel Ltd is a Ghanaian-owned limited liability company dedicated to delivering authentic, well-curated travel experiences across Ghana and Africa. We specialize in cultural, heritage, leisure, and experiential tourism designed to connect travelers to Africa at its source.

Our work goes beyond sightseeing. We create journeys rooted in history, culture, and lived experience — journeys that allow travelers to engage meaningfully with places, people, and stories that define Africa.

"Africa is not just a destination — it is a beginning. And every journey should start where it all began."

With strong local knowledge and professional coordination, we serve individuals, groups, institutions, and organizations seeking travel that is intentional, safe, and enriching.
